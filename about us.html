About Us

Buttercup Bakes is a student-run business that started in 2023. We are passionate about creating delicious homemade treats that bring joy to our customers. Our mission is to provide high-quality baked goods that are made with love and care. We believe that treats should not only taste amazing but also make you feel at home. Whether you're craving a sweet indulgence or a savory delight, Buttercup Bakes has something for everyone. Join us on our journey as we continue to spread happiness one bite at a time.

Vision

Our vision at Buttercup Bakes is to become the go-to destination for delicious homemade treats in our community. We aim to create a warm and welcoming environment where customers can enjoy our delectable baked goods and feel like they are part of our family. We envision expanding our product range to include a variety of treats that cater to different tastes and dietary preferences. Our ultimate goal is to bring joy and comfort to our customers through our delicious creations, making every bite a delightful experience.

Mission

Our mission at Buttercup Bakes is to provide our customers with high-quality, homemade treats that are made with love and care. We are committed to using the finest ingredients and traditional baking techniques to create delicious baked goods that bring joy to our customers. We strive to create a welcoming and friendly atmosphere where customers can enjoy our treats and feel like they are part of our family. Our mission is to spread happiness one bite at a time and make every customer feel special with our delectable creations.
